# Projection Properties of a Dot Product # For a given unit vector $\hat{a}$, project onto $b$. $\hat{a} \cdot b$ produces the *signed* length that can be scaled by $\hat{a}$ to produce a vector that is the length of $b$, but with the orientation of $\hat{a}$. $p = \hat{a} * (\hat{a} \cdot b)$
